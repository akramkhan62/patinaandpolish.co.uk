Philosophy

The Balance of Old & New

"Every period property tells a story. My role is to honor that history while ensuring your home serves your life today."

I believe in respecting the patina—the natural aging and character that makes period properties special. The marks of time, the settled plaster, the worn floorboards. These aren't flaws to be erased; they're features to be preserved.

But preservation doesn't mean living in a museum. The polish comes from knowing which modern improvements enhance rather than diminish. Which materials breathe with old walls. Which colors honor Victorian intentions while feeling fresh today.

After 20+ years working with period properties, I've learned that the best results come from understanding—not fighting—the bones of these beautiful homes.

— Akram Khan, Founder
